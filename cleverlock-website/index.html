No more keys or combos. Just your fingerprint.

Clever is a smart, keyless padlock that opens and closes with your fingerprint. Just touch the sensor on its face to lock and unlock. Equipped with advanced biometric sensor and Bluetooth technology, you'll never worry about clunky keys or obscure combinations ever again. Clever supports multiple users and fingerprints for shared use and can be charged via micro USB. Setup and configuration is easy with the Clever App—available free on iOS and Android.

STRONG AND BEAUTIFUL

Premium all-metal unibody construction with stainless steel core and gorgeous anodized aluminum shell.

ADVANCED BIOMETRICS

State of the art FPC OneTouch® biometric sensor technology accurately verifies your fingerprint in less than a second.

iOS & ANDROID

Wireless setup and management is easy using any Bluetooth-enabled iOS or Android device through the official Clever App.

DIGITALLY SECURE

With encrpyted data frames over low-power Bluetooth, only hardware running the official Clever app with authorized users may connect.

MULTI-USER

Stores up to 32 fingerprints over 4 users for shared use. Easily manage user permissions and view access history.

SWING SHACKLE

With a unique Swing Shackle mechanism, Clever can open and close laterally without any vertical displacement. Low profile for tight spaces.

Versatile, for just about anywhere.


With a 5mm diameter shackle and dimensions of 80mm x 40mm x 20mm (including shackle), Clever is sliim fits virtually anywhere: travel luggage, gym bags, lockers and more.

Clever is also FCC and IC certified, so it is safe for in-flight use and around your other electronic devices (IC: 22591-305001BF; FCC ID: 2ALPA305001BF).
